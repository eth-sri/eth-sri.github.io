About me

As of July 2024, I am a Ph.D. student at the Secure, Reliable, and Intelligent Systems Lab of ETH Zürich and advised by Prof. Martin Vechev. My research revolves around the reliability and security of Large Language Models, focusing on faithfulness and correctness of generated text and code. Previously I worked on formal verification of data structures and algorithms, and co-founded a Start-Up in the field of Smart Contracts.
