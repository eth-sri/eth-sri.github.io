About me

I am a Scientist in the Software Systems Security group at the ABB Corporate Research Center. I completed my PhD degree in the Secure, Reliable, and Intelligent Systems Lab, supervised by Prof. Martin Vechev. My research focuses on static analysis techniques for relaxed memory models and software security.
