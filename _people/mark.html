About me

Mark N. Müller is a senior Ph.D. student at the Secure, Reliable, and Intelligent Systems Lab of ETH Zürich and advised by Prof. Martin Vechev. Mark’s research focuses on provable guarantees for machine learning models. This includes both deterministic and probabilistic certification methods for a diverse range of architectures and certified training methods. He has led the research on these topics at SRI Lab for the last two years, including two industry collaborations, co-organized the Verification of Neural Networks Competition 2022 (VNN-Comp’22), and was the lead organizer for the 2nd Workshop on Formal Verification of Machine Learning (WFVML'23) at ICML’23 this year.
