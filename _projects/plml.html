--- title: "BigCode: Statistical Programming Engines" ref: plml image: programming.png description: "This project combines programming languages and machine learning for building statistical programming engines: systems built on top of machine learning models of large codebases. These kinds of engines can provide statistically likely solutions to problems that are difficult or impossible to solve with traditional techniques." ---
