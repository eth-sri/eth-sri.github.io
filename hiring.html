We are always looking for talented candidates to join our lab. Successful applicants should have a strong commitment to research and enjoy working at the intersection of theory and practice.

For up to date information on our research projects, please see our Research page.

Interested candidates should send a CV to Prof. Dr. Martin Vechev.

More details about the positions:

  • ETH is one of the top computer science departments in the world: CS University Rankings
  • Full scholarships are available for outstanding B.Sc. / M.Sc. students interested in the PhD.
  • A PhD/post-doc position has attractive salary (e.g. 70-90K CHF/year) and social benefits.
  • A position is for a maximum of 6 years.
  • Zurich is consistently ranked among the top destinations in the world for quality of life.
  • Doctoral studies at ETH: http://www.ethz.ch/doctorate/index_EN
